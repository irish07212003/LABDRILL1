      Frequently Asked Questions

      1. How do I navigate the website?

      Simply click on the navigation buttons at the top of the page (Home, Menu, Order Online, About Us, Contact Us, FAQ, Blog) to switch between different sections.

      2. How do I place an order?

      - Go to the Menu page.
      - Click the "Add to Cart" button for the items you want.
      - Click the "Order Online" button in the navigation bar.
      - Fill out the order form with your name, delivery address, phone number, and payment method.
      - Click "Place Order" to complete your order.

      3. What payment methods do you accept?

      • Cash on Delivery
      • Credit/Debit Card
      • GCash

      4. How do I know if my order was placed successfully?

      Once you submit your order, you will see a confirmation message displaying your order details.

      5. How can I contact customer support?

      Email: contact@bitebliss.com
      Phone: +63 912 345 6789
      Address: 123 Food Street, Manila, Philippines

      6. How do I send a message or inquiry?

      - Go to the Contact Us page.
      - Fill out the form with your name, email, and message.
      - Click "Send Message" and wait for a confirmation message.

      7. My order form isn‚Äôt working. What should I do?

      Make sure all required fields are filled in correctly. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page or contacting customer support.

      8. Why is my cart empty after adding items?

      Currently, the cart only displays items temporarily. If you navigate away from the Menu page, your cart items may not be saved. We recommend adding all items before proceeding to the Order Online section.

      9. Do you offer delivery services?

      Yes, we deliver to select areas. You will be notified about the estimated delivery time upon order confirmation.

      10. Can I modify my order after placing it?

      Currently, modifications are not available after submitting an order. If you need to change something, please contact us immediately.
